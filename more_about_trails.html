At this page I will try in a few words describe my emotions and experience at the trails of BC. Also all these information will serve as memory "database" for me and my family. As you know, our brain is able to forget everythisg very fast, so this page is for refreshing good memory and sharing part of my life with you, my dear friends.
Admiralty point trail

It was one of my very first trails, here in BC. Very warm microclimate of Belcarra made that winter weather much softer. We walked toghether with our friends, Uliana and Steve, and their dog Kalie ( she is adorable).

Crystal Falls trail

We 've discovered this trail accidentally. Nice trail along the river with fall at the end.

Buntzen Lake loop trail

10 km loop trail around the lake. We came there with a big company of friends and have lots of outdoor entertainments on the beach.

PoCo loop trail

I've done this trail by parts and, of course, by bike. 28 km loop trail around the city of Port Coquitlam (PoCo), my current location.

PoMo Shoreline trail

Remarkable Easter trail. Kids were lookind for Easter eggs from Easter bunny.

Jug island Beach trail

Wonderful trail along the ocean. My daughter found star fish between the rocks. She was really excited.

Iona Jetty trail

Nice and windy trail almost near the Vancouver International Airport in Delta. So all our walk we were observing the arrival and departure of airplanes.

Stanley Park Seawall trail

We've done that trail a couple of times and I like it a lot. So close to big vibrant city and so tight to the nature. My favourite place is Second beach and rocky part of trail.

Golden Creek Falls trail

Amazing place, deep inside of mountains. Still very chilly, but quiet and relaxed. Mountain creek and waterfall, huge first grow logs.

Shannon Basin loop trail

One of the longest trail we've done with the kids, more than 10 km, high in the mountains. We found a lot of snow, despite it was the beginning of summer. We walked through all creeks that joined together to make a huge waterfall

U.B.C. Malcolm Knapp Research Forest trail

Our first trail with high elevation gain and also long one. Found a lot of berry bushes and amazing views.

Lightning Lake loop trail

Trail around the lake, while we were camping. Turquoise color of water, ground squirrels and pine trees.

Hicks Lake trail

Trail around the lake and tons of salmonberries, we couldn`t stop eating them, huge, red and sweet berries. White and black slugs and, of course, geese with goslings.
